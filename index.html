About

When finding the right designer can be a challenge, I aim to provide multidisciplinary value to employers. In 2021 I received an MFA in Design Research and development from The Ohio State University. I have 3+ years in UX working in subject matter areas including real estate tech, federal health and defense consulting.

I love indie comics, dungeons and dragons, drag queens and am a proud enrolled member of the Choctaw Nation of Oklahoma.
